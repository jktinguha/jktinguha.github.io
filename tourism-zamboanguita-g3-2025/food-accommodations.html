Local Cuisines

Tabios

A type of legume known as pigeon peas, locally called tabios in Zamboanguita and kadyos in other parts of Negros.

Sinakol

A native delicacy made from glutinous rice (sticky rice) mixed with coconut milk and sugar, then wrapped in banana leaves and steamed.

Torta

A sweet, fluffy sponge cake made with flour, sugar, eggs, and lard, often flavored with anise or vanilla.
